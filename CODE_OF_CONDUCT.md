# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in the AI Fairness Platform a harassment-free
experience for everyone, with particular commitment to the values of fairness,
inclusion, and responsible AI development.

## Standards
- Respectful, constructive technical discussion
- Rigorous citation of original research
- Honest reporting of benchmark results
- Prioritizing the welfare of people affected by biased AI systems

## Enforcement
Report violations to conduct@fairness-platform.dev.

Adapted from the Contributor Covenant 2.1.
